9 ways to Slow The Effects

Global Warming is primarily caused by burning fossil fuels like oil and coal. By taking a few simple steps, you can help save our planet and money.

  • Reduce. Reuse. Recycle. Use only what you need. Consider energy efficient and recycled products.
  • Travel smarter Bike, carpool, or walk to cut down on vehicle emissions and gasoline purchases.
  • Eat Organic and Local Buying local helps reduce emissions, and fresh foods take less energy to produce.
  • Turn Off The Lights Turn off unused lights. Switch to energy efficient settings on appliances.
  • Switch to CFL or LED Lights One bulb will use use 2/3 less energy and save you money.
  • Use Smarter Adjust home temps to burn less energy. Use less hot water on dishes and clothes.
  • Clean and Replace Air Filters Make your systems more energy efficient by keeping the dust traps clear of debris.
  • Plant a Tree Help eliminate existing carbon dioxide in the air and keep the plant green.
  • Raise your voice Bring attention to global warming and spread the word to friends and family.
